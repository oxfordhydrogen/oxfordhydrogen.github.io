About Us

Oxford Hydrogen, founded by Andrew Murphy, pioneers catalytic processes that simultaneously advance the hydrogen economy and specialty chemical sectors. By integrating breakthrough catalysis with diverse feedstocks, we address decarbonisation and value creation in a single platform.

Our Vision

We aim to reshape how clean hydrogen integrates into global value chains — not as an isolated commodity, but as a catalyst for sustainable innovation powering multiple industries of the future.

Technology Highlights

Low Energy

Our process consumes significantly less energy than traditional reforming or electrolysis, cutting energy costs and improving efficiency for industrial adoption.

High-Value Co-Products

Simultaneously produces valuable chemicals used in pharmaceuticals, energy storage, advanced materials, and green chemistry markets, boosting project economics.

Scalable & Modular

Designed for modular deployment — from laboratory validation to full-scale industrial production — without complex re-engineering of processes.
